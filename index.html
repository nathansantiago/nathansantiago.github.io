About Me

I'm a Computer Science student studying at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. My programming journey began with making games on my DS and older brother's TI-84 using BASIC. From there I began exploring game development in Unity with C#. I continue to view programming as a creative outlet to express our wildest dreams. Over the last year I've shifted my focus from game development to web development and data visualization. I want to create web apps that have the potential to improve our everyday lives. I am passionate about game development, web applications, and data visualization.
